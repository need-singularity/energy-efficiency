# Plünnecke-Ruzsa Doubling at k+ℓ=n=6

**Entry**: `MILL-DFS24-09-plunnecke-6` (atlas.n6)

**Statement**: Plünnecke-Ruzsa: |kA − ℓA| ≤ K^{k+ℓ} |A| where
K = |A+A|/|A|. For k+ℓ = 6 (e.g. k=ℓ=3, or 4+2, 5+1) the doubling
exponent is 6, giving |3A−3A| ≤ K⁶ |A|.

**Verdict**: TEXTBOOK INSTANCE — no new bound. Recorded in n6atlas only
because the exponent equals 6, the atlas pivot index. No claim that
n=6 is structurally distinguished within the Plünnecke hierarchy.
