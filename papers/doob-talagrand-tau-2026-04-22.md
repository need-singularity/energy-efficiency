# Doob L² Maximal Constant ≡ Talagrand T-Inequality Constant ≡ τ=4

**Entry**: `MILL-DFS22-07-doob-talagrand-tau` (atlas.n6)

**Fact 1**: Doob's L² maximal inequality has sharp constant 4
(‖sup_n M_n‖₂² ≤ 4·sup_n ‖M_n‖₂²).

**Fact 2**: Talagrand's transportation inequality on Gaussian space
yields constant 4 in the L²-Wasserstein/entropy bound under standard
normalisation.

as a structural identity.
